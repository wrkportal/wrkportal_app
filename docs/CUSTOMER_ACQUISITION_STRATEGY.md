## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Customer Acquisition

### 1. **Start with Free Tier**
- Low barrier to entry
- Users can try before buying
- Word-of-mouth marketing
- Build user base quickly

### 2. **Focus on Value, Not Features**
- Don't just list features
- Show how it solves problems
- Use case studies and testimonials
- "Save 20 hours/week" not "10 new features"

### 3. **Target Small Businesses First**
- Lower sales cycle (1-2 weeks vs 3-6 months)
- Less competition (everyone targets enterprises)
- Faster to close deals
- Build case studies faster

### 4. **Leverage Social Proof**
- Customer testimonials on homepage
- "Join 1,000+ teams using [Your App]"
- Case studies with real companies
- User reviews (G2, Capterra, Product Hunt)

### 5. **Create Urgency**
- Limited-time discounts
- Feature gating ("Upgrade to unlock")
- Growth limits ("Need more users? Upgrade")
