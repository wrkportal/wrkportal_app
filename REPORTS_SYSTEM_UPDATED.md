## â“ FAQ

### Q: What's the difference between Live Reporting and Scheduled Reports?

**A:**

- **Live Reporting** = Real-time data from database (automatic)
- **Scheduled Reports** = Files you upload (manual)

### Q: Do I need to upload files to the system?

**A:** Only if you want to! Live Reporting works automatically without uploads.

### Q: Can I edit uploaded reports?

**A:** No, but you can delete and re-upload updated versions.

### Q: How do templates work?

**A:** Templates are blank forms. You create them, download, fill out, then upload to Scheduled Reports.

### Q: Can I create templates in the app?

**A:** Yes! Define fields and format in the app, then download the blank template.

### Q: What file types are supported for upload?

**A:** Excel (.xlsx), PowerPoint (.pptx), PDF (.pdf), and Power BI (.pbix)

### Q: How long are uploaded reports stored?

**A:** Indefinitely, until manually deleted.

### Q: Can I schedule automatic uploads?

**A:** Not yet - coming in Phase 2 with email automation.
